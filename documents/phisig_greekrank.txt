I’m not really sure what nice things to say about Phi Sig. I went to Drexel for five years and was very involved in Greek Life and Phi Sig was that sorority that you see in movies that bullies the protagonist, except they don’t learn a lesson at the end about friendship or whatever. To state facts over feelings, Phi Sig has the highest rate of disaffiliation on campus, a consistently average/low GPA, and one of the lowest rates of participation in Panhellenic (in terms of delegates, committee members and Rho Gammas). I would say of all of the sororities on campus, Phi Sig has the weakest ties to their philanthropy. They don’t have a large philanthropy event like the other chapters and they don’t focus heavily on raising money. If you are looking for a sorority where the main focus is ~aesthetic~ then you’ll love Phi Sig. If your main concern is Instagram followers and getting likes on that bikini pic you took in Cabo, then Phi Sig is for you. The frats seem to like them a lot so there’s that for you as well. If you want to be surrounded by people who look exactly like you, sure why not. They play a great social media game, but all I ever heard from Drexel Phi Sigs was how catty their sisters were and how much they all wanted to drop. Honestly if you have no personality beyond liking The Bachelor and worrying about your Instagram follower count but you look amazing in a crop top, you’re perfect and you’ll love it.
